# 📱 Social Media Do’s and Don’ts Checklist

## ✅ Do’s

### 👍 Think Before You Post  
Take a moment to pause and ask yourself: *Would I be okay if anyone saw this later?*  

### 🔒 Keep Your Privacy Safe  
Only share what you’re comfortable with. Some things are better kept personal.  

### ✔️ Check Before You Share  
Make sure the information is accurate so you don’t unintentionally spread false content.  

### 🤝 Be Kind and Respectful  
Treat people online the same way you would in real life—with respect and understanding.  

### 🌱 Show Your Best Side  
Share content that reflects you positively—your ideas, skills, and achievements.  


## ❌ Don’ts

### 🚫 Don’t Share Personal Details  
Avoid posting sensitive information like your phone number, address, or anything too private.  

### ⚠️ Don’t Post Hurtful Content  
If something might offend or harm someone, it’s better not to post it.  

### 💬 Avoid Getting Into Arguments  
Not every comment needs a reply—sometimes it’s best to ignore and move on.  

### ❌ Don’t Spread False Information  
If you’re unsure about something, don’t share it.  

### 🙅 Don’t Overshare  
You don’t need to post everything—keeping some parts of your life private is completely okay.  


## 💡 Final Tip

👉 *Post like your future self is watching—because one day, it probably will.*
